Incentive Solutions B2B eBook

What is Incentive Solutions?

Incentive Solutions is a B2B SaaS software that specializes in channel managment and corporate incentive programs.

In this case study, I will be sharing my process and rationale behind some of my decisions that I made for the design for this eBook

Reading

First thing I do when I get content I design around is I read it, this is a very important step and helps me understand what I'm designing around, and how I can make my design as impactful as possible.

BRAND PERSONALITY

For this eBook I wanted to inject a large amount of branding and overall story telling into the book. My idea was to tell the story of ROI (the company's mascot) to fly into space and land onto the moon. I was hoping the imagery of ROI flying in a rocket would show association between our software and increasing or "launching" if you will our potential clients ROI.

Layout

When ever I begin designing a book or any graphic project that's content heavy I start with blocking all of the content. It allows me to see how the pages will flow and interact as well as show me where I can add some flourishes and anything else extra.

Illustrating

After I finsih the blocking I start working on where I'd want to place some illustatrions and start sketching out how I want them to look.

Coloring

Once I finish illustrating I start mixing in colors on the pages, I do this after illustrating so that I can see what colors will work with the colors of the imagery.
